01 — About

Building at the intersection of hardware & software

I'm a Product R&D Engineer Intern at Zipbolt Innovation and B.E. ECE student at Thapar Institute. My work sits at the edges: where a mechanical constraint meets an electrical one, or where sensor data needs to travel reliably across a rover chassis on Mars-like terrain.

I specialize in the full hardware loop — schematic capture, custom PCB fabrication in Altium, embedded firmware, SolidWorks enclosures, and field validation. I'm also the Overall Student Coordinator at the Mechatronics and Robotics Society, directing 60+ students across the International Rover and Space Drone competitions.

3 conference papers. 4 custom PCBs. 70+ 3D-printed parts. Two international podiums. Still building.
